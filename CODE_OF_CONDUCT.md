# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, contributors and
maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community

Examples of unacceptable behaviour:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting

## Enforcement

This is a small personal project. Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise
unacceptable behaviour may be reported privately to the maintainer at
**privacy@spelix.app**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and
will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the
circumstances. The maintainer reserves the right to remove comments, commits,
issues, and contributions that violate this Code of Conduct, and to block
violators.
